Usage

  1. Visit a webpage with a video, for example, this.
  2. Click the video and play it if the video doesn't play automatically.
  3. Press shortcut keys when you are playing a video.

If a video in <iframe> tag plays automatically, you have to click the video to enable shortcut keys.

If the webpage has the same shortcut key as that of this extension, you have to change the shortcut key of this extension.

Default shortcut keys and their functions

  • d : increases the playback rate
  • a : decreases the playback rate
  • s : sets the playback rate as default
  • c : advances 10 seconds
  • z : rewinds 10 seconds
  • e : increses the volume
  • q : decreases the volume
  • w : sets the volume as default
  • r : shows the controller on the top-left of the video
  • t : reloads the settings without reloading the webpage
